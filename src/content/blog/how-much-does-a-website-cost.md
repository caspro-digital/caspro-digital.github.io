If you're a small business owner looking to build or redesign your website, the first question on your mind is probably: *how much is this going to cost?*

The answer depends on what you need, but we believe in transparency — so here's a real breakdown.

## The Short Answer

Most small business websites cost between **$1,500 and $10,000** to build, depending on complexity. Add **$150–$500/month** for ongoing management, hosting, and optimization.

## What Affects the Price?

Several factors influence the total cost of a website project:

- **Number of pages** — A 5-page site costs less than a 15-page site
- **Custom functionality** — Booking systems, e-commerce, and custom integrations add complexity
- **Content creation** — Do you have copy and images ready, or do we need to create them?
- **SEO requirements** — Basic SEO is included in every build, but advanced SEO strategy is an ongoing investment
